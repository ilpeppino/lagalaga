This Privacy Policy explains how LagaLaga ("LagaLaga," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal information when you use the LagaLaga mobile app, APIs, and related services (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

Category Examples Source
Account and profile information Internal user ID, Roblox user ID, Roblox username/display name, Roblox profile URL, avatar URL, login timestamps From Roblox OAuth and from your use of the Service

  • Create, manage, and display gaming sessions and invites.
  • Operate friend and social features.
  • Improve reliability, performance, and security.
  • Detect, prevent, and investigate abuse, fraud, and policy violations.
  • Communicate with you about service updates, account matters, and support requests.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

4. How We Share Information

We may share information with:

  • Service providers/processors that help us run the Service (for example, hosting, database, logging/observability, and notifications).
