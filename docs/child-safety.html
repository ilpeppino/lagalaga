Child Safety & CSAE Policy

Last updated: [2026-02-20]
Zero tolerance. LagaLaga prohibits child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) and child sexual abuse material (CSAM).

1. Prohibited content and behavior

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or any sexual content involving minors
  • Grooming, solicitation, or sexual exploitation of minors
  • Requests for sexual content from minors or offers to trade it
  • Any attempt to facilitate, normalize, or distribute CSAE/CSAM

2. Reporting

If you see suspicious behavior or content, report it immediately.

  • Email: lagalaga@gtemp1.com (use subject: Child Safety Report)

3. Enforcement

When we detect or receive credible reports of CSAE/CSAM we may:

  • Remove content and suspend or terminate accounts
  • Preserve relevant records where legally permitted/required
  • Report to appropriate law enforcement and/or child protection authorities where legally required

4. Cooperation with authorities

LagaLaga cooperates with law enforcement and relevant child protection organizations in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

5. Contact

For child safety concerns: lagalaga@gtemp1.com
