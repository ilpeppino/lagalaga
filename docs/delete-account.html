Delete Your LagaLaga Account

You are in control of your personal data. If you decide to stop using LagaLaga, you can request permanent account deletion at any time using the app or the web-based fallback below.

Delete your account in the app

Use the built-in account deletion flow in the LagaLaga app:

Settings → Account → Delete Account

Request account deletion from the web

If you cannot access the app, you can request deletion by contacting support at support@lagalaga.com.

Please include the following details so we can verify and process your request:

  • Username
  • Registered email
  • User ID (if known)

What happens when you delete your account

  • Your account profile is deleted.
  • Sessions you created are deleted.
  • Friend associations are removed.
  • Active tokens are revoked.

Data that may be retained

Certain limited records may be retained only when legally required or necessary for legitimate security purposes, including:

  • Security logs
  • Fraud prevention records
  • Information required to meet legal obligations

Deletion timeframe

Deletion is initiated immediately and completed within 30 days.
