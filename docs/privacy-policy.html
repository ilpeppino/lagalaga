Privacy Policy

Last updated: [2026-02-20]

This Privacy Policy explains how LagaLaga collects, uses, and protects information when you use the app.

1. Information we collect

  • Account data: Roblox-linked identifiers (e.g., username/display name) used to create your LagaLaga profile.
  • Session data: sessions you create or join (title, participants, time, visibility).
  • Device & diagnostics: basic diagnostics (app version, error logs) to keep the service reliable.
  • Push tokens (optional): if you enable notifications, we store a push token to deliver them.

2. How we use your information

  • Provide core features (sessions, invites, friends)
  • Maintain security and prevent abuse
  • Improve reliability (error monitoring, performance metrics)
  • Send notifications you opt into (session reminders, invites)

3. Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only when necessary to:

  • Provide the service (e.g., infrastructure providers)
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect users and prevent fraud/abuse

4. Data retention

We retain data only as long as needed for the app to function and for legitimate business and legal purposes. You may request account deletion.

5. Your choices

  • Request account deletion via in-app flow (if available) or by emailing lagalaga@gtemp1.com.
  • Disable notifications in device settings.

6. Contact

Questions or requests: lagalaga@gtemp1.com
